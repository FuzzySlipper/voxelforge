# Commercial Products: Voxels + AI (2023–2026)

## Voxel-Specific Products

### VoxAI
- **Link**: https://www.voxelai.ai/ | [Blog](https://blog.voxelai.ai/)
- **What it does**: AI-powered text-to-3D voxel model generator. Text/image → voxel model in ~2 minutes.
- **Features**:
  - Prompt expansion and reference image preview before generation
  - Adjustable cube size (low-res Minecraft style to hi-res Teardown style)
  - Export formats: .vox (MagicaVoxel), .obj, .glb, .json
  - Lighting, scaling, color blending controls
  - Cube merging, interior fill, color consolidation for polygon optimization
- **Generation types**:
  - Text-to-Image: 4 voxel art images (20 credits)
  - Image-to-Image Voxel: 4 voxel art variations (20 credits)
  - Image-to-3D: full 3D voxel model (25 credits)
- **Pricing** (2025):
  - Starter: $9/mo — 250 credits (~7 models)
  - Pro: $14/mo — 1,000 credits (~28 models)
  - Premium: $49/mo — 5,000 credits (~140 models)
- **Target market**: Minecraft creators, game developers, voxel artists

### Eques + Sega: Voxel Monster Generation
- **Link**: https://www.gamedeveloper.com/business/sega-teams-with-ai-startup-eques-on-voxel-generation-tool | [VGC](https://www.videogameschronicle.com/news/sega-and-an-ai-startup-have-created-tech-that-uses-ai-to-generate-voxel-monsters/)
- **What it does**: AI tool generating voxel monsters from text descriptions. Born from University of Tokyo startup Eques, partnered with Sega.
- **How it works**: Players describe a monster ("alligator, sharp teeth, wings") and the tool generates a matching voxel creature in Minecraft/Roblox art style.
- **Goal**: User-generated content (UGC) for Sega games
- **Status**: Unveiled at JIKEI COM Game and eSports Show (Japan, October 2025). Not yet publicly available.

### VoxiGen
- **Link**: https://voxigen.io/en
- **What it does**: Free browser-based voxel editor with AI generation from text/images. Selected for Web Summit ALPHA program.
- **Target**: Indie game devs, hobbyists, educators

### 3D AI Studio (Voxel Mode)
- **Link**: https://www.3daistudio.com/blog/creating-3d-pixelized-models-a-quick-guide-to-voxel-modeling-with-3d-ai-studio
- **What it does**: General AI 3D generation with a voxel/pixelized output mode.

## Text-to-3D Services (Voxel-Adjacent)

These generate meshes/NeRFs but can be part of a voxel pipeline:

### Meshy AI
- **Link**: https://www.meshy.ai/
- **What it does**: Text/image to 3D model generation API. Has MCP server integration. Outputs meshes that can be voxelized.

### Tripo3D
- **Link**: https://www.tripo3d.ai/
- **What it does**: AI-powered 3D model generation with voxel conversion guide/tools.

### Luma AI (Genie)
- **What it does**: Text-to-3D and image-to-3D generation. Mesh output.

### CSM (Common Sense Machines)
- **What it does**: Image/video to 3D model generation.

## Manufacturing/Industrial (Using "Voxel" in Name)

### Vixiv (formerly Voxel)
- **Link**: https://www.additivemanufacturing.media/articles/voxel-developing-ai-alternative-to-generative-design
- **What it does**: AI-powered additive manufacturing design platform. Multi-physics AI model for rapidly generating designs for high-performance 3D-printed parts. Based in Cincinnati, OH.
- **Relevance**: Uses voxel-based internal representations for physics simulation, not creative voxel art.

### Voxel (Safety Platform) — Unrelated
- **Link**: https://www.voxelai.com/
- **What it does**: AI workplace safety platform using computer vision. Raised $44M Series B. Not related to 3D voxel generation.

## Key Observations

1. **VoxAI is the only dedicated commercial text-to-voxel product** with a mature pricing model
2. **Eques/Sega** represents the biggest gaming industry investment in AI voxel generation
3. Most commercial 3D generation tools treat voxels as a format conversion step, not a native representation
4. The market for voxel-specific AI tools is still very small compared to mesh-based 3D generation
